About Me

Hey! I'm Justin. I love all things science and outdoors. On week days I can usually be found performing tests in an ultra-high vacuum chamber where I aim electron beams at electrically conductive or insulating materials and study how they arc, spark, glow, ...etc. On weekends, however, I am frequently found hiking and enjoying fresh air with my wife and dog, woodworking, learning some new programming concept, or playing music on the piano, guitar, or violin.
