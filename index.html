About Me

Hey! I'm Justin. I love all things science and outdoors. I studied physics at Utah State University where I received my Master's degree in 2017. I chose this field because I wanted to learn about everything from the small particles we're all made out of, to the planets, stars, and gallaxies hurling around our universe, and everything inbetween. Along the way, I've learned a lot about programming in order to extract intriguing information from huge heaps of data, as well as to streamline repetetive processes to make life easier for me and my fellow researchers.

My first language is C++, which I love because learning it teaches a lot about the inner workings of computers, as well as how to represent and handle any real life object in your program. I've also had classes in school that used Fortran and Matlab, and I've taken time on my own to dable with c#, Java, Ruby, html, css, and Javascript. Programming is such a blast, because it allows you to build from scratch almost anything you can imagine,
